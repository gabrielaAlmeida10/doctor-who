9th Doctor

Surviving the Last Great Time War, and believing he was the only survivior, the Ninth Doctor is living in grieving over the immense suffering he had witnessed and the destruction he had caused with his actions. Struggling with the fact that he is the last of the Time Lords

The Doctor is now alone and bereaved. Emotionally haunted by the war, he entered a darker period of his life spattered by resentment, where he tended to brood and act rudely to those who rubbed him the wrong way, and found a particular rage towards the Daleks, he believed to be desroyed whenever he encountered them. Despite this, he remained jovial and friendly towards anyone he took a liking to.
